Başak Güleçyüz

I am a PhD candidate at the Chair of Media Technology at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), supervised by Prof. Dr.-Ing. Eckehard Steinbach. My research lies at the intersection of robotics, machine learning, and haptics. In particular, I study how robots can be taught contact-rich manipulation skills through teleoperation, with a focus on learning from demonstration, shared autonomy, and haptic communication. My work addresses the challenges of remote skill transfer under real network constraints, including delay, packet loss, and limited bandwidth, by combining learning-based methods with haptic coding and passivity-based control.
